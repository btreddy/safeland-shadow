SSP Private Limited is a process engineering company based in Faridabad, Haryana, India, specializing in turnkey projects for industries like food, dairy, chemicals, and effluent treatment. Established in 1977, it focuses on innovative, energy-efficient solutions such as evaporation and drying systems.
​

Company Overview
SSP Private Limited provides customized turnkey solutions, equipment, and process innovations for food processing, dairy alternatives, starch, salt refining, herbal extraction, and zero-liquid discharge effluent treatment plants. The company employs 501-1,000 people and holds certifications reflecting quality and compliance standards. CEO is Tapas Chatterjee, with GST No. 06AAECS1415J2ZJ and CIN U29219HR1985PTC021129.

History and Growth
Founded on August 29, 1977, SSP began with stainless steel pipes and dairy equipment before expanding into evaporators and spray dryers in the 1980s. Key milestones include establishing an R&D center in 1987 (DSIR-recognized), first exports in 1995, and developing zero-liquid discharge systems in 1998 for pharmaceuticals and other sectors. Manufacturing units expanded to locations like Butibori, Noida, Sitarganj, and Chandpur, Faridabad.

Key Achievements
SSP has supplied over 500 evaporation systems, 700 dryer systems, and 350 extraction systems to more than 1,500 clients in 64 countries, with over 50% repeat orders. Exports reach regions including Africa, CIS, Latin America, Europe, and the Middle East, including projects for Amul Dairy. The company emphasizes R&D with 10 patented technologies and pilot clusters for sustainable process optimization.

Major Projects in India
SSP has executed numerous turnkey installations across dairy derivatives, food/beverage processing, and zero-liquid discharge solutions, with over 50% repeat orders from Indian clients. Specific examples include dairy projects for major cooperatives and starch/salt refining plants in multiple states. Their Faridabad base supports local manufacturing for these initiatives.

SSP Private Limited has advanced sustainability in India through zero-liquid discharge (ZLD) effluent treatment plants, energy-efficient evaporation and drying systems, and water recovery technologies for industries like dairy, pharma, distilleries, and textiles.

ZLD and Effluent Treatment
SSP pioneered ZLD systems since 1998, installing plants for pharmaceutical, chemical, and distillery sectors to achieve 100% wastewater recycling and compliance with strict environmental norms. These projects minimize water usage and enable waste valorization, serving clients in states like Haryana, Maharashtra, and Uttar Pradesh.

Energy-Efficient Processes
The company supplies multi-effect evaporators and spray dryers that cut energy consumption by up to 50% in dairy (e.g., Amul) and food processing, reducing carbon footprints via steam economy and cogeneration integration. Innovations include herbal extraction and salt refining plants promoting resource efficiency.

Broader Impact
Over 1,500 Indian installations emphasize sustainable process engineering, with R&D yielding 10+ patents for low-emission drying and extraction tailored to local regulations. SSP's Faridabad facilities support eco-friendly manufacturing for nationwide projects.
